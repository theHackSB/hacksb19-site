About

What is Hack SB?

Hack SB is a 12 hour student organized hackathon for high school students.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where groups of enthusiastic people come together to build awesome things in a limited period of time. At a hackathon, experienced engineers and interested beginners will learn new skills, build and innovative solutions for real-world problems, and share their creation with the other participants as well as the judges, all in one day. If you want to learn more, see the MLH Hackathon FAQ.

FAQ

Who can attend?

All high school students are welcome, even if you do not attend SBHS. If you are a college student, and interested in mentoring, please contact us! Please direct all mentoring inquiries to sbcompsciclub@gmail.com

Do I need any prior experience?

Not at all! The less experience you have, the more you can learn!

Can I win anything?

Of course! At HackSB, you will have the opportunity to compete with others and win many prizes

What should I bring?

You will need:
  • Laptop
  • Phone
  • Chargers
  • Powerstrips
  • Paperwork
  • Imagination
