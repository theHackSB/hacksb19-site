What is Hack SB?

Hack SB is a student organized hackathon for high school students.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where groups of enthusiastic people come together to build awesome things in a limited period of time. At a hackathon, experienced engineers and interested beginners alike will learn new things, build innovative solutions to real-world problems, and share their creation with the community, all in one day. If you want to learn more, see the MLH Hackathon FAQ.
