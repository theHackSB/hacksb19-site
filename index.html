About

What is Hack SB?

Hack SB is a 12 hour student organized hackathon for high school students.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where groups of enthusiastic people come together to build awesome things in a limited period of time. At a hackathon, experienced engineers and interested beginners will learn new skills, build and innovative solutions for real-world problems, and share their creation with the other participants as well as the judges, all in one day. If you want to learn more, see the MLH Hackathon FAQ.

FAQ

Do I need a South Brunsiwck library card or registration to attend the event?

No, you dont need a library registration to attend Hack SB all you need are the paperwork sent to your inbox.

Can I or my friend participate without registration for hackSB?

Yes - but only if a dosent show up and if you have the required paperwork. We highly recommend you sign up before hand.

I dont know how to code, can I still come.

Of course, hackSB is tailored for both newcomers and experts. You can attend workshops to get introduced with certian topics and make your first program. In addition, you may seek help from the mentors.

What do I need to bring?

You will need:
  • A computer (with admin access)
  • Your computers charger
  • Power extension
  • Your paperwork
